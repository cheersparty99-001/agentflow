FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How fast can I get started with Flowreach?
We set everything up for you within 24-48 hours. You'll describe your ideal customer, connect your email, and we handle the rest. Your first qualified leads will be ready to outreach within your first week.
Where do the leads come from?
We source directly from Google Maps, then enrich with verified emails and phone numbers from public databases. Every lead is real, operational, and matched to your ICP — no scraped LinkedIn ghosts.
Is cold outreach via Flowreach compliant with anti-spam laws?
Yes. Flowreach enforces volume caps and respects PDPA (Malaysia), GDPR (Europe), and CAN-SPAM (US). We only contact business email addresses with a legitimate B2B purpose.
Can I use my existing Gmail or Outlook account?
Yes. Connect your Gmail account with one click via OAuth. Outlook support is coming soon.
What happens if I cancel?
Cancel anytime — just reach out to us and we'll handle it within 24 hours. You keep access until the end of your billing cycle.
