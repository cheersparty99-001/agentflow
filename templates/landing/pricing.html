FAQ

Pricing questions, answered

Everything you need to know before you sign up.

What is your money-back guarantee?
If you're not satisfied with the lead quality within the first 7 days, contact us for a full refund. We're confident in our results.
Can I cancel anytime? What happens to my data?
Cancel from your dashboard in two clicks — no calls, no forms, no retention pitches. You keep full access until the end of your billing period, and you can export every lead, conversation, and metric to CSV before or after cancellation. We hold your data for 90 days post-cancellation in case you come back, then it's permanently deleted.
Which outreach channels are supported?
All plans include both email (Gmail, Outlook, custom SMTP) and WhatsApp Business outreach. Email runs via OAuth with automatic warm-up and deliverability tuning. WhatsApp uses the official Business API with template approval handled for you. SMS and LinkedIn outreach are on the roadmap for late 2026.
Is my data safe? Where is it stored?
Yes. Flowreach is hosted on enterprise-grade infrastructure in Singapore (AWS ap-southeast-1) with end-to-end TLS encryption in transit and AES-256 at rest. We are PDPA-compliant (Malaysia), GDPR-ready, and SOC 2 Type II audit is in progress for Q3 2026. We never train AI models on your lead data, conversations, or campaign content.
Which languages does the AI write in?
Flowreach's AI writes natively in English, Bahasa Malaysia, and Simplified Chinese (中文) — tone-matched to the recipient's industry and business size. The agent auto-detects which language a prospect is most likely to engage with based on their business name, location, and online presence. Manual override is always available.
What industries does Flowreach work best for?
We see the strongest results in B2B service businesses targeting SMEs: F&B operators, retail chains, logistics & freight, hospitality, professional services (legal, accounting, marketing), fintech, SaaS, and B2B distributors. If your ideal customer has a physical location or a Google Business profile, Flowreach can find them. Pure-play consumer (B2C) and enterprise (Fortune 500) use cases are not our sweet spot.
Where do the leads come from?
Leads are sourced directly from Google Maps and Google Places APIs, then enriched with verified emails, phone numbers, and business profile data from public B2B databases. Every lead is a real, operational business in your specified geography and industry — no LinkedIn scrapes, no resold lists, no dead contacts. Each lead also gets a 1–10 fit score so you focus only on the prospects worth pursuing.
Do you offer custom or enterprise plans?
Yes. For teams needing more than 800 leads or 3,000 messages a month, multi-region campaigns, white-label deployments, on-prem hosting, custom SLA, or dedicated infrastructure — drop us a line at yy@flowreach.work. Enterprise plans typically start at RM 4,000/month and include a named customer success manager plus quarterly business reviews.
What's your refund policy?
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on your first paid month. If Flowreach isn't delivering value, email yy@flowreach.work within 14 days of your first charge and we'll refund the full amount — no questions asked. Yearly plans are refundable on a pro-rata basis within the first 30 days.
Can I upgrade, downgrade, or pause my plan?
Anytime, from your dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately with pro-rated billing. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle so you keep what you paid for. You can also pause your account for up to 60 days per year — your data is preserved, your integrations stay connected, and billing stops until you resume.
