Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Flowreach Sdn. Bhd. ("Flowreach", "we", "us", or "our") operates the Flowreach sales automation platform at flowreach.work. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. What Data We Collect

When you use Flowreach — whether by booking a demo, signing in, or using the platform — we may collect the following personal data:

  • Email address — used for account creation and outreach
  • Company name — used to understand your business and tailor our service
  • WhatsApp number — provided when booking a demo or configuring outreach
  • Account credentials — hashed passwords and session data for secure login
  • Lead data — business information you import or generate through the platform (company names, contact details, outreach history)

We do not collect sensitive personal data (such as race, religion, or health information) unless you voluntarily provide it.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to deliver and improve the Flowreach service:

  • To process demo requests and communicate with you about your account
  • To operate the sales automation platform (lead discovery, outreach, pipeline tracking)
  • To send service-related communications (billing, feature updates, support)
  • To improve platform performance, security, and user experience

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not train AI models on your lead data, conversations, or campaign content.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your data is hosted on Supabase, which uses cloud infrastructure in:

  • Singapore (AWS ap-southeast-1) — primary data storage
  • United States (AWS us-east-1) — backup and secondary processing

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). We maintain strict access controls and regularly review our security posture.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. After cancellation, we hold your data for 90 days in case you choose to resume your subscription. After this period, your data is permanently deleted from our systems.

You may request earlier deletion at any time (see Section 6).

5. Your Rights (PDPA Compliance)

Flowreach complies with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA) of Malaysia. As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Deletion — request that we delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw your consent to data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at yy@flowreach.work. We will respond within 30 days as required by PDPA.

6. Data Deletion Requests

If you would like us to delete your personal data, send a request to yy@flowreach.work with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will verify your identity and process the deletion within 14 business days. Some data may be retained for legal or compliance obligations (e.g., billing records).

7. Third-Party Services

Flowreach uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Google Maps / Places API — lead discovery
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Railway — cloud hosting

Each third party has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data. We only engage providers with adequate security and compliance standards.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or through the platform. Continued use of Flowreach after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact us at:

Email: yy@flowreach.work
